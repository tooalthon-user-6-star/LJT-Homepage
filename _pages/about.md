# About Me

I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~hunkim starred repositories BILLIONS/nlp.html), where I am supervised by [Professor Junxian He](https://cse.hkust.edu.hk/~jhe/). My research focuses on **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Machine Learning**, with specific interests in:

- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability
